Learning Gamified

Learning Gamified brings to users learning in a fun interactive way. Games are available for both school and college students. The gamification of learning motivates students to learn by capturing their interest and by maximizing enjoyment. It is a very effective form of learning as it involves a fun sort of commitment from the learners.

College students require learning more advanced topics. In this section, they can find complex topics and have the freedom to test those out. For example they can make encryption fun by passing texts to convert to either Morse code or to apply the RSA algorithm. They can also test out how machine learning works by passing pictures for the website to analyse.
